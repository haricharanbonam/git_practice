What is ISTE?

1941

Established Legacy

Started as APTI in 1941, became ISTE in 1968. A national level non-profitable society.

1.24L+

Massive Community

Over 1,24,000 professionals across India - teachers, students, and industry experts.

28

Years at SRKR

28+ years of empowering students at SRKR with the motto: "Believe in yourself and your ability to succeed."
